About

Here thoughtful administration meets seamless execution

I’m Charlie, the founder of Charlathan VA.

I provide tailored and refined administrative and event planning support to entrepreneurs and small businesses who are ready to step out of the overwhelm of administrative exhaustion and into a more supported, streamlined way of working.

I believe that behind every successful business is a well-run backend—and that’s where I thrive. I specialize in managing the details that quietly hold everything together: inboxes that stay organized, schedules that flow smoothly, clients who feel cared for, and events that are thoughtfully planned from start to finish.

My approach is intentional, calm, and detail-driven. I take the time to understand your business, your priorities, and your vision so the support I provide feels seamless, aligned and personalized—not transactional. Whether I’m assisting with day-to-day operations, social media support, or planning an in-person or virtual event, my goal is to create ease, structure, and consistency behind the scenes.

What truly sets my work apart is the blend of administrative excellence and event planning expertise, paired with a high-touch client experience. I believe support should feel elevated, reliable, and personal—like having someone in your corner who anticipates your every need, and handles the details with the care they deserve.

I work best with clients who value thoughtful collaboration, clear communication, and a supportive partnership rooted in trust. If you’re looking for someone who treats your business with the same level of care and intention as you do, you’re in the right place.
